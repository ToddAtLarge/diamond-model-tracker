Diamond corners

Adversary — top vertex. Who is behind the activity.
Capability — left vertex. Tools, malware, TTPs used.
Infrastructure — right vertex. IPs, domains, C2, delivery mechanisms.
Victim — bottom vertex. Target person, org, or asset.

Each diamond is one discrete event. Connect diamonds to build an activity-attack graph showing how the adversary pivoted across an intrusion.

To link two events: click Connect, then click a colored corner dot on the first diamond (e.g. Infrastructure), then a corner dot on the second. The link is anchored to those exact features — click it again to label or delete.
